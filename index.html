FAQ

Do I need a fishing license?

Most areas require a state fishing license for participants over a certain age. We’ll help you find the right link for your state.

Where do we meet?

We use local parks, lakes, and piers with safe, easy access. We’ll confirm a spot when you book.

What if the weather is bad?

Light rain is fine; heavy rain or unsafe conditions will be rescheduled at no charge.

Do you provide gear?

For lessons, basic gear is available to borrow. You’re welcome to bring your own rod and reel.
