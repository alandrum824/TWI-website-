What We Do

First Catch Lesson

Step-by-step shore lesson for beginners. Learn casting, knots, baiting, and safe handling. Perfect for kids and families.

Kids’ Fishing Day

Fun, safety-first fishing designed just for kids (with parents/guardians). Casting games and fish stories included.

Family Adventures

Bring the whole family for a relaxed morning on the shore. Chairs, shade, and plenty of laughs provided.

Texas Know-How

More than just fishing—you’ll hear Texas stories, life lessons, and outdoor tips passed down the old-fashioned way.

Meet Your Guide

Born in Corpus Christi and now living in Fort Worth, your guide is a proud dad and granddad with a heart for teaching. He’s known for his patience, humor, and passion for the outdoors. With him, you’ll learn not just how to fish, but how to enjoy a true Texas day by the water—family, fun, and a few tall tales included.
