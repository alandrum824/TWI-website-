FAQ

Do we need a license?

Most adults need a Texas fishing license. We’ll point you to Texas Parks & Wildlife.

Where do we meet?

Marine Creek Lake by default; also Lake Worth and Eagle Mountain Lake. Exact spot confirmed after booking.

What about weather?

Light rain is fine; heavy rain or unsafe conditions will be rescheduled at no charge.

Do you provide gear?

Basic gear is available to borrow during lessons. Bring your own if you like.
