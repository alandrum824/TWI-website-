Born in Corpus Christi and now living in Marine Creek Ranch, Fort Worth, your guide is a southern gentleman with a heart for teaching. As a dad and granddad, he’s known for his humor, patience, and Texas hospitality. With him, you’ll learn not just how to fish, but how to share stories, laugh often, and enjoy a real Texas day by the water.
