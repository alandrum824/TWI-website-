❓ FAQ

🎣 Do we need a fishing license?

Most adults need a Texas fishing license. We'll point you to Texas Parks & Wildlife for easy online purchase!

📍 Where do we meet?

Marine Creek Lake by default; also Lake Worth and Eagle Mountain Lake. Exact spot confirmed after booking with detailed directions!

🌦️ What about weather?

Light rain is fine; heavy rain, storms, or unsafe conditions will be rescheduled at no charge. Safety first!

🎣 Do you provide gear?

Basic gear is available to borrow during lessons. Feel free to bring your own if you prefer!

👶 What ages work best?

Kids 5+ with a parent/guardian do great! We adapt our teaching style to each child's attention span and interests.
